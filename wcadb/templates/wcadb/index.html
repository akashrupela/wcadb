WCA-PostGres

Welcome to my website built on WCA Database using Django and PostgreSQL. You can browse following pages:
  • Rankings - See global and national world rankings for all WCA Events.
  • Records - See world records and country/continental records.
  • Competitions - See details of past and upcoming competitions.
  • Battle - Perform a comparison of two competitors' performances in WCA competitions.
  • Other statistics - See other assorted miscallenous statistics on the WCA database.
